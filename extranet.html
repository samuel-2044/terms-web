Extranet

An extranet is a private network that uses Internet technology to share part of a business's information or operations with external parties, such as suppliers, vendors, partners, or customers.

Unlike an intranet, which is restricted to an organization's internal users, an extranet allows controlled access to authorized external users. It is typically secured with firewalls, authentication, and encryption to protect sensitive data.
